# Business Model — StorePulse

## Problem

60% of F&B businesses in Singapore fail within the first year. The #1 cause: wrong location.
New business owners rely on gut feel and expensive ($10K+) traditional consultants. No affordable, data-driven alternative exists for SMEs in Southeast Asia.

## Solution

StorePulse democratizes location intelligence via a live web platform showing:
- Store density heatmaps by type (14 categories = 14 demographic signals)
- Historical opening/closure trends (Time Machine)
- Dead zones (high historical closure rate = risky bet)
- Cross-category ratios as socioeconomic signals (coffee:convenience = office:residential)

---

## Revenue Model (4 Tiers)

### Tier 1 — District Brief
**SGD 300–500 one-time**

- 1 district, 1 store category focus
- Density analysis + dead zone check
- 3–5 page PDF with map screenshots
- Delivered in 48 hours
- Target: first-time F&B entrepreneur, franchise scout

### Tier 2 — Full Location Report
**SGD 1,500–3,000 one-time**

- 1–3 specific addresses evaluated
- Full signal matrix (14 categories cross-analysed)
- Competitor proximity analysis
- Historical 3-year trend data
- 10–15 page PDF with Mapbox visualizations
- Delivered in 5 business days
- Target: SME expanding 2nd location, retail brand entering Singapore

### Tier 3 — Strategic Study
**SGD 5,000–12,000 one-time**

- Full district or planning area study
- Optimal zone recommendation for a specific business type
- Dead zone risk scoring
- HDB supply gap analysis
- Market entry timing recommendation
- 20–30 page report + 1 live Q&A session
- Target: franchise brand entering Singapore, property developer, PE-backed retail chain

### Tier 4 — Retainer
**SGD 2,000–5,000 / month**

- Monthly signal refresh reports
- Alert when new dead zone clusters form
- New district opening analysis on request
- Quarterly strategic update
- Target: chain with 10+ locations, franchise developer, REIT asset manager
